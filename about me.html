Hiking

I love being able to disconnect from reality for a little and be surrounded by nature. I have a whole journal dedicated to my favorite trails in the North GA mountains. At a young age my dad would always take me hiking during the weekends. I have tried to carry on the tradition now as I am in my late 20's.

Before my dog Leia passed away she would always love to enjoy the trails with me, being able to jump in the water was her favorite part.During her lifetime we were able to explore over 50 trails together.


I have 3 endeavors in hiking to do before I turn 50

  1. Hike Mt.Rushmore
  2. Be able to explore an underwater cave
  3. Explore Yosemite National Park



My Pets

Having a pet(or a few) is always a must in my household. I tend to think that pets are better then most people. Growing up I would always try to bring in strays. I even tried to "rescue" some tadpoles I found in a stream because I thought they didn't have enough water.


As of now I have 2 kittens that we adopted from Windy Ridge Rescue. Our kittens have their own little set up in our gaming room but they love to just sit in our laps and watch us play. We also have a crawfish, he goes by Rob the Lob.
